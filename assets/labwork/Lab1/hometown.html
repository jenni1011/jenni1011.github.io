City Information

Hong Kong is a Special Administrative Region (SAR) of China located on the eastern side of the Pearl River estuary in East Asia. It is known for its skyscrapers, shopping, and cultural fusion of East and West. Hong Kong is a major global financial center and a popular tourist destination. The official languages are Chinese (Cantonese) and English.
