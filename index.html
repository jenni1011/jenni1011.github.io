Information about Algonquin College:
Algonquin College is a public college located in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. It was established in 1967 and has since become one of the largest colleges in Ontario, with over 20,000 full-time students and 36,000 part-time students.

Algonquin College offers a wide range of programs in various fields, including business, media, engineering, health sciences, hospitality, tourism, and information technology. The college also offers programs in apprenticeship training and continuing education.
